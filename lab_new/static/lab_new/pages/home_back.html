Welcome

Welcome to Dr. An-Yuan Guo's Lab at the Huazhong University of Science and Technology.

Our research interests mainly focus on:

TF and miRNA co-regulation in complex diseases;

Lsarge-scale data integration, database construction, and tools to addressing bioinfomatics problems;

Cancer genomics: take advantage of the data from genome projects and high-throughput technology to investigate biological mechanisms of cancer development, key regulators in cancer as well as find biomarkers and drug targets in specific cancer.
